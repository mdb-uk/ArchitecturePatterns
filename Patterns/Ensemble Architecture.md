# Ensemble Architecture


Historically the decisions about AI usage were limited to "traditional" AI (Machine Learning or Deep Learning) or LLMs (Large language models, encoder/decoder etc).

The ensemble technique allows you to leverage multiple AI models of a variety of types to use the advantages of each of them.

Traditional AI looks at structured data, and then following a set of rules, makes some predictions. They are smaller in size, less latency and less power requirements.

LLM encoders work similarly to Traditional AIs, but they use larger models so are less energy efficient and have more latency, but are more accurate. Decoder models work on unstructured data and generate new content from the unstructured training data.

Hybrid ensemble models pick the beneficial aspects of these various types of models and combine their strengths
