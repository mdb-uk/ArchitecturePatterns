# Agentic planning

Allows agents to create plans and strategies for dealing with tasks and problems.

- Input
- Decomposition - breaking the task down into smaller parts
- Plan selection and execution
- Reflection and refinement
- Output

This pattern involves a structured loop of the above steps where the AI works towards completing objectives, continuously updating their strategy based on the outputs from the steps above.

Techniques involved:

- ReAct (Reasoning and Acting) - involves the AI reasoning about the steps needed to achieve a goal and then acting on those steps
- ReWOO (reasoning without observation) - allows the AI to plan and execute tasks without needing to observe immediate outcomes, relying instead on predefined strategies
